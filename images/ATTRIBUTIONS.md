## Adding New Images

When adding new images to this book:

1. **Verify the license** is compatible (CC0, CC BY, CC BY-SA, or CC BY-NC-SA)
2. **Download highest quality** available
3. **Save to appropriate folder:**
   - `screenshots/` for Ubuntu screenshots
   - `illustrations/` for diagrams and SVGs
   - `photos/` for hardware/stock photos
4. **Add attribution below** with all required information
5. **Update this file** with proper attribution
